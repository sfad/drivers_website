Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

Last Updated: August 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Find Your Ride collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our transportation marketplace platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Profile photos and identification documents
  • Emergency contact information

Location Information

  • GPS coordinates for route optimization and tracking
  • Pickup and drop-off locations
  • Home and work addresses

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Trip history and transportation patterns
  • Device information and identifiers
  • Log files and analytics data

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To connect passengers with drivers and facilitate transportation services
  • Safety & Security: To verify identities, conduct background checks, and ensure platform safety
  • Communication: To send notifications about rides, updates, and important announcements
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Information Sharing

We respect that passenger and dependent information is personal and belongs to the passenger (or the passenger's account owner). We do not sell or otherwise provide passenger personal data to third parties for their independent use. We share data only in the limited circumstances described below and only to the extent necessary to provide the service or to comply with legal obligations.

  • With Drivers (minimal trip data): We share only the information necessary for a driver to complete a booked trip (e.g., passenger name, pickup/drop-off locations, contact number for the trip). Sensitive passenger profile details that are not required to operate the trip are not shared with drivers.
  • With Organizations: When you book through an organization's transportation service, we may share trip-related information with that organization strictly for trip coordination and reporting. Organizations do not receive passenger profile data beyond what is required for the booking unless you explicitly consent.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who perform services on our behalf (for example: maps, push notifications, analytics, cloud storage). These providers are contractually required to protect personal data and use it only to provide services to us.
  • Legal Requirements: We will disclose personal data when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users. We limit disclosures to the minimum necessary to meet the legal requirement.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales, we may transfer personal data as part of the transaction, but we will require the recipient to honor this Privacy Policy and only use personal data for the purposes disclosed here.

Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with the following third-party services. Note: we do not currently process payments or integrate with payment processors. If we add payment processing in the future, this section will be updated with details.

  • Maps & Navigation: Google Maps for location services
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for usage analytics
  • Communication: Firebase for push notifications
  • Cloud Storage: AWS for secure data storage

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with industry standards
  • Limited access controls and employee training
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@findyouride.com

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for users 13 years and older. For users under 18, parental consent is required. We implement special protections for minors and comply with COPPA requirements for children under 13 who may use our service through family accounts.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Trip data is typically retained for 7 years for legal and safety purposes, while profile data is retained while your account is active plus 3 years after deactivation.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or app notification. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
